Backstory
There was once a time, long before humans came into existence, when only the elements existed. At first, these elements remained in a state of delicate balance but it wasn't long before chaos erupted. No one knows when it happened, but at some point the elements started to become greedy. Fire, earth, water and air began to fight against each other in order to try and further their own existence. Eventually these elements manifested into physical beings known as mages. To this day, in the darkest areas of the earth, these mages still do battle for the sole purpose of total dominance....

Mage Rage: The 4 Elements
Upon joining a new game you will be randomly assigned one of 4 random elemental mages. These mages are equipped with the power to summon powerful beams of concentrated energy. Should these beams collide, at chaotic effect will occur.

Controls
W=Up
A=Left
S=Down
D=Right
Space=Shoot

Beam Collisions
Air + Earth = Tornado
Tornadoes have erratic movement. If a player comes in contact with a tornado she will be spun around and flung across the game map.

Air + Fire = Lightning.
Lightning will spread out an arbitrary distance from its point of generation. If lightning hits a player, she will be damaged and there is a chance that it may arc to the next closest player.

Air + Water = Ice.
A sheet of ice will be generated for an arbitrary period of time. A player moving on ice will have lessened control.

Earth + Fire = Lava.
Lava shall flow for an arbitrary period of time, damaging any player that comes into contact with it.

Earth + Water = Mud.
A portion of the game map will become mired in mud for some period of time. Any player coming into contact will be significantly slowed.

Fire + Water = Steam.
A cloud of super hot steam will be generated; the cloud will drift around the game map for some amount of time. Any player coming in contact with the cloud will be damaged.
